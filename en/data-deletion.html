How to Delete Your Account and Data?

We respect your privacy. If you want to delete your data from the Adsum app, you can do so at any time.

Instructions:
  • Send an email requesting data deletion.
  • In the message, provide your first and last name used in the app (or your user identifier).
  • Your data will be permanently deleted from the Firestore database within 30 days.

Send your request to:

przemyslaw.biz@gmail.com

Please note that after data deletion, it will not be possible to recover access to your trip history or chats.
