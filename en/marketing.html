Attendance Management in Seconds

The perfect attendance management app for trip organizers, teachers, guides, and event coordinators. Forget paper lists and time-consuming attendance checks – count your group in seconds!

Key Features

Lightning-Fast Attendance

Start a roll call and see in real-time who's present, who's on the way, and who's missing. Participants confirm their attendance with a single tap.

Easy Group Joining

Share a unique code or QR code with your group. Participants join instantly by scanning or entering the code – no registration required.

Announcements

Inform everyone with instant announcements. Every participant receives a push notification immediately.

Trip Schedule

Create a detailed schedule with dates, times, and locations. Participants always know what's next.

Built-in Chat

Group chat for the entire trip and private messages between participants and organizers. Stay connected throughout the journey.

Co-organizers

Invite co-organizers to help manage the trip. Share responsibilities for attendance counting and sending announcements.

Participants Without App

Add participants who don't have smartphones. Mark their attendance manually during roll calls.

PDF Export

Generate QR codes for printing and easy distribution.
